Managing the Tribblix firewall
==============================

The firewall in illumos is ipfilter. At present, zap provides a basic
interface to manage ipfilter, allowing you to enable, disable, and
check the status of ipfliter, with the following subcommands:

    zap manage-firewall status
    zap manage-firewall enable
    zap manage-firewall disable

To learn more about the various components of ipfilter, the relevant
parts of the manual can be accessed with the following commands:

    man ipnat.4i
    man ipf.5
    man ipmon.5
    man ipnat.5
    man ippool.5
    man ipfilter.7
    man ipf.8
    man ipfs.8
    man ipfstat.8
    man ipmon.8
    man ipnat.8
    man ippool.8
